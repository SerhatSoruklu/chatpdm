# RMG Non-Goals

RMG will not:

- perform open-world forecasting
- predict company collapse or recovery
- act as a prediction engine
- act as an intelligence engine
- generate narrative conclusions disguised as analysis
- use hidden judgment to fill evidence gaps
- fabricate probabilities or probability-like confidence theater
- browse autonomously
- ingest uncontrolled external data
- interact with external systems
- modify external systems
- replace LLGS concept resolution
- infer unsupported causal bridges from weak evidence
RMG is not a general research agent. It is not a freeform scenario generator. It does not decide what will happen in the world. It only governs whether a scenario query is admissible within explicit rules and evidence boundaries.
