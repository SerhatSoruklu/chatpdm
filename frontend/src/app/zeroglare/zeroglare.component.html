Zeroglare

Zero noise. Pure signal.

Zeroglare detects lexical and framing pressure signals before meaning construction. It reduces rhetorical noise, isolates semantic signal, and prevents ambiguity from spreading into deterministic resolution.

Zeroglare ensures the system responds to meaning, not presentation.
