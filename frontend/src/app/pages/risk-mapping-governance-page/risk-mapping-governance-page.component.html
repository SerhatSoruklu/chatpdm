Transparency / trust / architecture

Risk Mapping Governance (RMG)

A deterministic system for admitting, constraining, and mapping supported risk scenarios under explicit evidence and domain rules.

RMG does not predict outcomes. It maps supported structural paths, explicit gaps, and bounded support confidence.

View audit surface Explore how it works

Current posture

Deterministic, bounded, evidence-backed, inspectable.

The system stays inside authored registries, explicit compatibility rules, replayable governance, and compact output.

Confidence meaning

Bounded support confidence, not prediction.

Confidence class values describe how strong the authored support is within current constraints. They do not describe likelihood.

Output surface

Compact, deterministic output with explicit unsupported bridges

The final resolve contract stays compact and string-based. Bounded support confidence is a class, not a probability.
