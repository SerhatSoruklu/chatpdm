About

A system where concepts must pass before they exist.

ChatPDM is a deterministic concept system. It does not generate answers. It admits concepts only if they survive structural validation.

Problem

Meaning fails when systems tolerate drift.

Most systems rely on language whose meaning shifts under pressure. Definitions blur, boundaries soften, and the system continues even when the concept no longer holds. The result is inconsistency, ambiguity, and structural drift.
