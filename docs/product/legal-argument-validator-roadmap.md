# Legal Argument Validator Roadmap

This document locks the canonical roadmap title and phase order for the Legal Argument Validator workstream.

It exists to prevent drift in naming, ordering, and scope framing while the detailed phase documents are authored.

This document locks:

- the roadmap title
- the phase identifiers
- the phase order
