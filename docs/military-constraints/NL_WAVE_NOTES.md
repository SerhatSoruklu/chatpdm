# NL Wave Notes

## Purpose

This note records the Netherlands wave as a separate national family layered on the shared INTL baseline.

## Boundary

- `NL_NATIONAL_BASE_V1` is national, not Anglosphere-derived.
- `NL_ROE_BASE_V1`, `NL_COMMAND_AUTHORITY_V1`, and `NL_DELEGATION_CHAIN_V1` are national baselines that reuse INTL ancestry where appropriate.
- `NL_AIRSPACE_CONTROL_V1` is the first bounded executable Netherlands domain pack.
- NATO or coalition dependencies are used only where explicitly declared in registry/DAG truth.

## Intent

The Netherlands wave exists to prove that the admitted architecture generalizes beyond the Anglosphere while remaining bounded, source-backed, and deterministic.
