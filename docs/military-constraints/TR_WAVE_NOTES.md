# TR Wave Notes

## Purpose

This note records the Turkey wave as a distinct national family layered on the shared INTL baseline.

## Boundary

- `TR_NATIONAL_BASE_V1` is national and controlled-divergence specific, not Anglosphere-derived.
- `TR_ROE_BASE_V1`, `TR_COMMAND_AUTHORITY_V1`, and `TR_DELEGATION_CHAIN_V1` are national baselines that reuse INTL ancestry where appropriate.
- `TR_AIRSPACE_CONTROL_V1` is the first bounded executable Turkey domain pack.
- NATO or coalition dependencies are not introduced unless explicitly declared in registry and DAG truth.

## Intent

The Turkey wave exists to prove that the architecture generalizes beyond the Anglosphere while remaining source-backed, bounded, and deterministic.
